Ultra-Premium Whole-Bean Coffee

Ember Elevation is a Canadian specialty coffee brand devoted exclusively to freshly roasted, whole-bean coffees grown at high elevation. Our coffees are sourced from farms that practice fair trade and organic cultivation, selected with restraint, and released in limited quantities — never blended for volume, never rushed, never produced for scale.
